Online Ordering



CREDIT CARD PROCESSING MADE SIMPLE

Online Ordering Platform
We provide restaurants with an online ordering platform that accepts web and mobile orders from customers. We easily convert your menu to an online menu ready for your customers to order directly from you. All orders are managed under the restaurant's policy, such as: days and hours of operation, delivery range, minimum per order, tax, credit cards that you accept, etc. We design your menu with an identical 'look and feel' to match your brand and help you add an "Online Ordering" button to your website so you start getting orders immediately. With Online Ordering, your restaurant will see an immediate increase in volume, greater market penetration, higher average ticket amounts, and lower net costs. Powerful Features.

POS Intergation
Online Orders Now puts the order into your POS and prints out the tickets while the customer is still online. It's exactly like having an invisible cashier taking orders.

Larger Tickets
Customers order an average of 30% more online than over the phone or at the counter since the customer feels more comfortable taking their time exploring the menu and building their order. Online Orders Now presents your entire menu to the customer including modifiers. Customers can easily find what they want and order it just the way they like it.

Automatic Upsells
The Online Orders Now system never forgets to ask customers if they’d like fries with that.

Order Accuracy
Online orders are more accurate since the customer enters the order themselves. Online Orders Now is built from your specific restaurant’s database for the highest level of order fidelity.

Delivery Area Mapping
Customers can see exactly where your restaurant is located and your delivery area with a single click.

Reduced Credit Card Fees
Online Orders Now is fully certified and backed by many Authorized Merchant Service Providers.

Multiple Location Support
If you have multiple locations, each location can have a unique set of menu items and prices while maintaining a single consistent online presence.

Administration Menu
Your manager account allows you to modify menu items and other settings at anytime.
• Change Pricing
• Temporarily remove an item
• Change Delivery Times

30 Day Guarantee
If you are not completely satisfied with you Online Orders Now Online Ordering, let us know within the first 30 days and we will refund your full payment.
